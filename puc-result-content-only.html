How to check your result

Steps to download your 2nd PUC marksheet

  • Go to karresults.nic.in or kseab.karnataka.gov.in
  • Click "Karnataka 2nd PUC Examination-1 Result 2026"
  • Enter your registration number and select your stream
  • Click submit — result appears on screen
  • Download and save your provisional marksheet
  • SMS: Type KAR12 REGNO → send to 56263
  • DigiLocker: Login → search KSEAB → download signed marksheet

What to do after the result?

Important next steps for students

College admissions

Download marksheet from DigiLocker — valid for all admissions and counselling.

Revaluation

Apply for revaluation through KSEAB portal once the process opens.

Exam 2 chance

Failed students get Exam 2 & Exam 3 with no exam fee this year.

Entrance exams

Prepare for KCET, JEE, NEET, CLAT, CUET for your preferred stream.
